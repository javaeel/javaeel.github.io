About us

About us

Welcome to Java Eel Maritime Logistics, a premier shipping and logistics provider headquartered in Qinzhou, China. With over a century of experience in the industry, we specialize in custom shipping and logistics solutions that utilize lesser used ports around the world. Our boutique services are designed to meet the unique needs of our customers, and we're committed to providing discreet, professional, and reliable services that exceed expectations.
Our roots go back to the late 1800s, when we were one of the largest exporters of Java eel in the world. As the eel population declined in Europe, we expanded our business and started shipping to other parts of the world. Today, we serve a wide range of industries, including food, electronics, automotive, and pharmaceuticals, and we have a presence in key ports in the West Indian Ocean and beyond.
At Java Eel Maritime Logistics, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative and efficient solutions that help our customers optimize their supply chain. Whether you need to transport perishable goods, hazardous materials, or oversized cargo, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done right. We're committed to sustainability and eco-friendly practices, and we work closely with our customers to minimize our carbon footprint and reduce waste.
We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our company and services. If you have any questions or would like to request a quote, please don't hesitate to contact us. Thank you for considering Java Eel Maritime Logistics as your trusted shipping and logistics partner.

Offset Shipping

We provide a service which allows a customer to take credit for shipping a product without actually shipping the product and then receive the similar quantity product at a later time

  • Take credit for shipping without actually shipping: Take credit for shipping your product without ever having to ship it. Simply provide us with the necessary information about your shipment, and we'll handle the rest. You can use this service to fulfill orders, demonstrate proof of shipment, or for other business purposes.
  • Receive similar quantity product at a later time: We'll store it in our secure warehouse or on ship until you're ready to receive it. When you're ready, we'll ship the same quantity of product back to you, at a time and location that's convenient for you.

Specialized Containers

We're proud to offer a unique solution that makes shipping people safer, more comfortable, and more efficient than ever before.

  • Our ventilated shipping containers are designed with people in mind, and feature high-quality ventilation systems that ensure a constant flow of fresh air throughout the container. This helps to prevent the buildup of harmful gases and ensures that everyone inside the container can breathe easily and comfortably.
  • In addition to ventilation, our shipping containers also feature rudimentary plumbing systems that provide basic hygiene and sanitation facilities for passengers. This includes a small sink, a toilet, and a shower, all of which are designed to be compact, efficient, and easy to use. These basic amenities ensure that passengers can stay clean and comfortable throughout their journey, no matter how long or challenging it may be.
